KisanX Privacy Policy

Xrone Tech Private Limited

Effective Date: June 10, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to KisanX, a mobile application owned and operated by Xrone Tech Private Limited. KisanX is designed as a farming expense tracker, digital note-keeping tool, and AI-powered farming assistant to help Indian farmers and their families make smarter agricultural decisions.

By downloading, installing, or using KisanX, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being fully transparent about how your data is used.

The latest version of this Privacy Policy is always available at:
https://rohangadakh.github.io/kisanx-privacy/

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Authentication Data: We collect your phone number for OTP verification. If you sign in via Google, we collect your name, email address, and Google profile photo URL.
  • Profile Information: Full name, email address, phone number, and regional details including State, District, Sub-District, and Village.
  • User-Generated Content: Farming expense records, crop notes, planting/harvesting dates, pesticide records, and farm/crop photos you upload.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect your approximate or precise location to provide local weather updates, crop recommendations, and market pricing insights.
  • Device Information: Device model, Android OS version, and unique device identifiers for performance monitoring.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with app features, collected via Firebase Analytics to help us improve the app.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect or store credit card, debit card, UPI, or any banking information on our servers.
  • We do NOT access your phone contacts or call logs.
  • We do NOT collect background location without your explicit consent.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Account & Sync: To authenticate your identity and sync your farming records across devices.
  • AI Farming Insights: To provide personalized advice using Google Gemini AI based on your notes and location data.
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and fix bugs to make KisanX better.
  • Subscription Management: To manage your KisanX Premium subscription through Google Play Billing.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your queries sent to support@xronetech.com .
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable Indian laws and regulations.

6. KisanX Premium Subscription and Billing

  • Free Trial: New users enjoy a 3-Month Free Trial of KisanX Premium at no cost.
  • Subscription Price: After the free trial, KisanX Premium is ₹199 per month, auto-renewing monthly.
  • Payment Processing: All payments are handled exclusively and securely by Google Play Billing. Xrone Tech Private Limited does NOT collect, store, process, or have access to your credit card numbers, debit card numbers, UPI IDs, bank account details, or any other financial information.
  • Managing Subscription: You can view, pause, or cancel your subscription at any time via Google Play Store → Subscriptions.
  • Free Trial Cancellation: You may cancel during the free trial period and will not be charged.
  • Refunds: Refund requests are handled by Google Play as per their Refund Policy.

7. Data Storage and Security

  • All user data is stored securely on Google Firebase Global Infrastructure.
  • Data in transit is protected using TLS (Transport Layer Security) encryption.
  • Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 encryption.
  • We implement Firebase Security Rules to prevent unauthorized access to your data.
  • Your data may be stored on Google servers located outside India as part of Google's global infrastructure. We ensure all data handling meets applicable Indian standards.
  • In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users as required by Indian law.

8. Data Retention

  • We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active.
  • If you request account deletion, all personal data will be permanently deleted within 7 days.
  • Transaction and financial records may be retained for up to 7 years as required under Indian financial and tax laws.
  • Anonymized, non-identifiable analytics data may be retained longer for app improvement purposes.

9. How to Delete Your Account and Data

You have the right to request complete deletion of your KisanX account and all associated personal data at any time. This process is free of charge.

Step 1: Send an email to support@xronetech.com
Step 2: Use the subject line exactly: "Account Deletion Request - KisanX"
Step 3: Include in your email:
  • Your registered phone number or email address
  • Confirmation that you want all data permanently deleted
Step 4: We will verify your identity and send a confirmation email within 48 hours.
Step 5: Your account and all data will be permanently deleted within 7 days of verification.

What data will be deleted:

  • Your account profile (name, phone number, email, location details)
  • All farming expense records you created
  • All notes and crop records
  • All photos and files uploaded to your account
  • Your app preferences and settings
Important: If you have an active KisanX Premium subscription, please cancel it via Google Play Store first before requesting account deletion. Deleting your account does NOT automatically cancel your Google Play subscription, and you may continue to be charged.
Note: Deleted data cannot be recovered after deletion is complete. Financial transaction records required by Indian law may be retained in anonymized form for up to 7 years.

10. Children's Privacy

KisanX is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information without parental consent, we will delete it immediately. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided data should contact us at support@xronetech.com.

11. Your Rights Under Indian Law (DPDP Act 2023)

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Deletion: Request permanent deletion of your data (see Section 9).
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time.
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: Contact our support team if you believe your data rights are being violated.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@xronetech.com. We will respond within 48 business hours.
